Flutter:
1️. Options:
Navigator 1.0 (Imperative Navigation)
Navigator 2.0 (Declarative Navigation)
go_router (third-party package,Built on Navigator 2.0, simplifies routing)
2️. Recommended Approach: go_router (Easier than Navigator 2.0, supports deep linking, redirections)

Android Native:
1️. Options: Android Navigation Component (NavController), Manual Fragment Transactions
2️. Recommended Approach: Android Navigation Component (NavController) (Simplifies back stack handling and deep linking)


Why Navigation Came into Picture?
Allows moving between screens in an app.
Manages back stack & deep linking efficiently.
Prevents manual fragment/activity management complexities.
Ensures consistent user flow across the application.
